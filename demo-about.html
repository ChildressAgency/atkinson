About Us

Introduction

Atkinson Aeronautics & Technology is a Service Disabled, Veteran-Owned Small Business which offers a broad range of technical services and aviation operations. Atkinson merged with Tappahannock Aviation in late 2014 to create a new area of growth and service for our customers. Our company is composed of three major areas of expertise and capabilities:

Technology Services

Flight Operations

Aircraft Maintenance

Location

Atkinson's headquarters are located in Fredericksburg, Virginia. We also have operations in Alaska, California, Texas, North Carolina, Virginia and Maryland.
