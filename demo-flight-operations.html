Flight Operations

Introduction

Atkinson Flying Services is veteran-owned, and our pilots are former Marine Corps aviators and combar veterans with decades of experience, as well as thousands of flight hours.

We currently operate a Piper Seneca II from the Tappahannock-Essex County Airport. This unique location is surrounded by some of the country's most influential cities and business centers.

As an FFA-certified passenger and cargo air carrier, we provide on-demand aircraft operations to all the United States. We also are available for special purpose flights, such as system demonstration for manned and unmanned aerial capabilities, test range chase or other support functions.
