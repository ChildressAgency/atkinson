Aircraft Maintenance

Introduction

We are a full service, general aviation maintenance facility offering maintenance, repair, overhaul and modification services for all small reciprocating engine aircraft and light turbine powered aircraft. Located at Tappohannock-Essex County Airport (KXSA), we are central to the Eastern Virginia, Southern Maryland, and Washington, D.C. areas. Bring your plane to us for servicing and we will give you a free airplane ride to and from yoiur home airport within 50nm, and at a very low rate thereafter.

Services are provided for:

  • All light civil aircraft
  • Engines (Recip. & Light Turbo): Lycoming, Continental, Franklin, Rotax, Pratt & Whitney
  • Propellers

Our Services Include:

  • Airframe inspection, maintenance, repair and modification
  • Meets AD requirements for Janitrol heater pressure decay test
  • Engine Maintenance, replacement and repair
  • Lowest shop rates in the area
  • Certified A&P and IA
  • AO emergency services available
  • Factory Certified Independent Rotax Maintenance Technician (IRMT)
